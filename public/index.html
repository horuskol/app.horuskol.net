Apps by HorusKol

A small collection of apps that I have put together for fun.

Bézier Curve Tool
A simple tool for drawing cubic Bézier curves and generating an SVG path specification.
Conway's Game of Life
Cellular automaton simulation, devised by John Horton Conway. Uses the Canvas API.
Poker Dice
5 dice - roll 'em and score points.
Tower of Hanoi
A puzzle game - move a tower of blocks from one peg to another. An experiment in moving elements with the Mouse and Touch APIs.
